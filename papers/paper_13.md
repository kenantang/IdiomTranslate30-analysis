## Abstract / Key Contribution

Compared GPT-3.5, GPT-4o mini, Gemini 1.5, Llama 3.1, and Qwen2 on idiom and simile interpretation
across multiple languages. Created new Persian-language evaluation datasets. Investigated CoT,
few-shot, and English-translation prompting strategies. Found idiom interpretation nearing
saturation for high-resource languages. Open-source models still struggle with low-resource similes.

## Methods

- Idiom interpretation as multiple-choice and free-text tasks
- Chain-of-thought vs. few-shot vs. translate-then-interpret prompting
- Error analysis by language family and idiom category
- Human evaluation of free-text explanations (accuracy, fluency)

## Relevance to IdiomTranslate30

- Gemini 1.5 is evaluated here — IdiomTranslate30 was generated by Gemini 3.0 Flash Preview,
  making this useful for understanding Gemini's figurative language capabilities
- Saturation finding for high-resource languages motivates analysis of whether
  IdiomTranslate30's quality varies by target language resource level
- Prompting strategy comparisons provide context for interpreting the three strategy differences
